A Little Extra Reading

While your back at HQ teaching the techies, or just bored in your free time, you may also want to look into something else to do:

The poem , Addiction by Cdkeimling is a wonderful read about a poet who became infatuated with his phone and slowly came to realize that his infatuation was too much.
If you're more into fictional novels, I mean entirely real and currently happening novels, you should check out The Rule of Three by Eric Walters. In this riveting novel, main character Adam must bring together his dismayed neighbourhood after a massive EMP destroys all technology.
And for when you simply don't want to read, you can watch a movie about robots, called WALL-E directed by Andrew Stanton, or if you don't know/care who that is, Pixar. The movie follows the titular character in his pursuit of love while he deals with the remains of earth and discovers the truth about the humans who destroyed it

If you would like something a little more intellectual for a change, you could also look into some websites such as this one all about cyberbullying and how to stop with it or deal with it. Or you could look at this one where the question of, how dependent are we, is questioned and disscused.
